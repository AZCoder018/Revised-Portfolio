About Me

I am a former attorney from New Jersey, where I practiced law for 8 years. In 2017, I moved to Phoenix, Arizona, to live and work among family and friends in the area. Currently, I am enrolled in the University of Arizona Coding Boot Camp. I am scheduled to earn my Certificate in Full Stack Web Development in late July of 2018.

Although I am being trained as a full stack developer, I am particularly focused on working with React, Java, JavaScript, MySQL, and MongoDB. I also have a strong interest in quality assurance and product testing.

My experience and thoroughness as an attorney, combined with my web development skills, would make me a great asset to any company that builds and maintains applications and websites involving, among other areas, legal research, legal self-help, business research, risk management, and cyber-security.
